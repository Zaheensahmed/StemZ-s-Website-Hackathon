About Us

StemZ is a non-profit organization that teaches beginners Js, Raspberry Pi, and other STEM skills. We are now running a series on the introduction to JavaScript. In that series, we build games while discovering new properties. My goal is to shortly cover Python, CSS, and HTML.

Courses We Offer

Microgreens

In this course, we covered the journey of growing Microgreens. Young seedlings of palatable vegetables and herbs are known as microgreens. Microgreens may be picked and consumed a week to 10 days after the cotyledon — a portion of the embryo within the seed — leaves have formed, in contrast to bigger herbs and vegetables that take weeks or months to mature.
